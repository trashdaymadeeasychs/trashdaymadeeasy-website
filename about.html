Our Story

About Trash Day Made Easy

A family-owned business built on trust, reliability, and making life a little easier for the communities we serve.

How It Started

A Real Problem. A Simple Idea.

Our story started during a very hectic season of life here in Charleston. With three little ones under two years old, even simple chores felt huge. Trash day kept sneaking up, and hauling bins to the curb in the dark or rain was one more thing weighing on our minds.

We kept thinking — there should be a simple service that just handles this. That real need became Trash Day Made Easy, built to give other families, property managers, and operators one less thing to worry about.

We're family owned and live in the communities we serve. What started as a residential backdoor-to-curb service in Charleston has grown into a full operator-focused platform — serving multifamily apartment communities, build-to-rent portfolios, HOA communities, short-term rental operators, and select residential homeowners across Charleston SC and the greater Atlanta GA metro.

Same friendly, reliable, photo-verified service in every market. Same commitment to showing up — rain or shine.

"We built this company because we needed it ourselves. If trash day was stressful for us, we knew it was stressful for everyone."

— Bryan Gray, Founder & Owner
Owner Bio

Meet the Founder

Bryan Gray

Founder & Owner — Trash Day Made Easy LLC

Bryan Gray is the founder and owner of Trash Day Made Easy LLC, a valet trash and waste operations company headquartered in North Charleston, SC. Bryan launched the company after experiencing firsthand the challenge of managing routine household tasks during a demanding season of life — with three young children, trash day became one chore too many.

That personal experience shaped the company's core mission: make waste management effortless, reliable, and professional for everyone — from busy homeowners to large-scale property operators. Under Bryan's leadership, Trash Day Made Easy has grown from a local residential service into a multi-market operator-focused platform serving multifamily properties, build-to-rent portfolios, HOA communities, and short-term rental operators across Charleston SC and Atlanta GA.

Bryan is committed to building a service that operators and residents can count on — every night, in every market, with photo-verified accountability on every visit.
