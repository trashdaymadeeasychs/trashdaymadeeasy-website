General

Valet trash service is a doorstep or backdoor trash collection offering where we come directly to your home or unit, pick up your bagged trash, and transport it to the dumpster or curb — so you never have to make that trip yourself. We offer two service types: Backdoor-to-Curb for residential homeowners, and Doorstep-to-Dumpster for multifamily apartment communities.
We currently serve two markets. In Charleston SC, we provide full service for multifamily properties, build-to-rent communities, STR operators, HOA communities, and residential homeowners — covering North Charleston, West Ashley, Mount Pleasant, Summerville, James Island, Daniel Island, and surrounding areas. In Atlanta GA, we serve multifamily, BTR, and STR operators across the metro, including Buckhead, Midtown, Sandy Springs, Alpharetta, Marietta, and Decatur.
Yes. Trash Day Made Easy is fully licensed and insured. All team members are background-checked before working in our service areas. We carry general liability insurance and operate as a registered LLC headquartered in North Charleston, SC.
Yes. We serve residential homeowners with our Backdoor-to-Curb bin valet service, and we serve property operators — including multifamily apartment communities, build-to-rent portfolios, HOA communities, and short-term rental operators — with our Doorstep-to-Dumpster and curbside management services.

For property operators, we'll discuss your property type, unit count, and service needs and build a custom plan.

Residential Service

On your municipal trash collection day, we come to your property and pull your trash and recycling bins from your backdoor or side yard to the curb before the truck arrives. After your bins have been emptied, we return them to their designated spot. You don't have to do anything — we handle it start to finish, every week.
We place bins at the curb early enough to ensure they're out before your municipal collection truck comes through. Collection schedules vary by neighborhood, so we time our route accordingly. If you have concerns about your specific collection time, just let us know when you sign up.
Service continues in all weather conditions — rain, wind, cold, and heat. That's part of what makes the service valuable. You don't have to go out in the dark or bad weather on collection morning. Our team is out there regardless.
Our Backdoor-to-Curb service means you don't need to put anything out — we come to wherever your bins are stored (backdoor, side yard, or designated bin area) and take it from there. There's nothing for you to remember or do on collection day.
We service standard residential trash and recycling bins provided by your municipality. If you have questions about specific bin sizes or extra bags, just reach out and we'll confirm what we can accommodate for your address.
No. You don't need to be home. We access your bins from their storage location, move them to the curb, and return them after collection — all without you needing to be present. Every visit is photo-documented so you have confirmation that service was completed.
No long-term contract is required. Residential service is available month-to-month with no cancellation penalty. We also offer annual billing at a discounted rate — annual billing gets you one month free. Cancel anytime with reasonable notice.
Residential bin valet service starts at $49/month for weekly service. Annual billing is available at a discount — pay for 11 months and get the 12th free. Pricing may vary based on service area and specific needs. Contact us for a quote for your address.

Multifamily & Operator Service

Residents place bagged trash outside their unit door by a designated time — typically 8PM. Our team arrives in the evening, collects from every door throughout the community, and transports all bags to your property's compactor or dumpster. Each visit is photo-confirmed. Your staff doesn't need to manage the process at all.
Collection schedule is set based on your property's needs and your dumpster or compactor pickup schedule. Most multifamily properties run nightly collection Sunday through Thursday or Monday through Friday. We work with you to set the right schedule for your community.
Yes. We work with property management companies and portfolio owners across multiple properties. We can service several communities under a single service agreement and provide consistent execution across all of them. Contact us to discuss your portfolio.
After each collection visit, our team documents service with photo confirmation. This gives property managers verifiable proof that service occurred at your property — useful for ownership reporting, resident disputes, and vendor accountability. You have a record of every visit.
Standard valet trash collection covers bagged household waste. Items that cannot be collected during a regular visit (oversized items, furniture, boxes) are noted and we can coordinate bulk item pickup as an add-on service. We'll flag any issues during our rounds so you're not left guessing.
Yes. We offer turnover trash handling as part of our operator services — removing trash and debris from units during make-ready between residents. This is especially popular with BTR operators and STR hosts who need fast, clean turns between occupants.
We work with properties of various sizes, from smaller boutique communities to large apartment complexes. There is no strict minimum — contact us with your property details and we'll let you know if it's a fit and what pricing looks like for your size.
Most properties are up and running within a few days of signing a service agreement. We'll set up your route, confirm scheduling, and brief your onsite team before the first collection night. The onboarding process is designed to be simple for property management teams.
