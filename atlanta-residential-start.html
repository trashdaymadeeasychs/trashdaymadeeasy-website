Frequently Asked Questions

Do you service all of Atlanta?
We are actively expanding throughout the Atlanta market. Submit your address using the form above and our team will confirm availability for your specific neighborhood.
Do I need to be home?
No. As long as we have access to your bins, you do not need to be home. Our team handles everything on your scheduled collection day.
Do you return the bins after pickup?
Yes. Service includes taking your bins to the curb before municipal collection and returning them afterward. You won't have to think about it.
Is there a contract?
No long-term contract is required. You can cancel anytime. We also offer annual billing with one month free for homeowners who want to lock in savings.
Can you help with both trash and recycling?
Yes. If recycling service is available in your area, we can help with both trash and recycling bins on your designated collection days.
